About The Ritual Co

The Ritual Co was born from the idea that fitness should feel like a celebration — not a chore. We believe that movement, much like meditation or music, is a ritual. It’s personal. It’s powerful. And it deserves design that reflects that.

Founded by Gitika Yadav, The Ritual Co blends softness and strength in every product — whether it’s our capsule-shaped dumbbells or our blush-toned mats. Built for women and wellness-forward minds, we want fitness gear to feel as good as it looks.

With India’s first pastel fitness collection, we’re reimagining home workouts to be calm, aesthetic, and consistent. We prioritize:

  • Ergonomic design for comfort and control
  • Pastel palettes that soothe and inspire
  • Thoughtful packaging and conscious material sourcing

Whether you're lifting dumbbells or lifting your mood, we’re here to remind you: Movement is a ritual worth honoring daily.
