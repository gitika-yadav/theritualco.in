Our story

We started with a simple problem: gym gear was either too harsh or too plain. We wanted equipment that would invite you to move—tools that match your space, your rhythm, and your mood.

The result is a collection that blends form and function: capsule-shaped dumbbells with a silk-matte finish, designed for intentional movement at home.

“I wanted fitness tools that don’t just sit in a corner—but inspire you to show up.” — Gitika Yadav, Founder

What we believe

  • Movement, not punishment

    Gentle progress > all-out intensity. Consistency builds strength.

  • Sustainability in practice

    Long-lasting designs, low-waste packaging, and mindful manufacturing partners.

  • Design that calms

    Soft pastels and clean lines—tools that reduce visual noise and encourage flow.

  • Strength for all

    Form-first weights that feel safe for beginners and empowering for everyone.
