Dumbbell for Beginners: Where to Start

Starting a strength training routine can feel overwhelming — but it doesn’t have to be. At The Ritual Co, we believe every fitness journey should begin with confidence, consistency, and the right tools.

Why 1kg or 2kg Dumbbells Are Ideal

When you're new, lighter weights allow you to focus on form and avoid injury. Our capsule dumbbells (available in 1kg and 2kg) are designed for beginners who want to tone, not bulk.

How to Use Dumbbells as a Beginner

  • Start with 10-minute routines, 3x per week
  • Use exercises like bicep curls, shoulder presses, and squats
  • Listen to your body and build gradually
