Top 5 Dumbbell Exercises for Arms

Want stronger, more toned arms without lifting heavy weights? These 5 dumbbell moves are beginner-approved and girl-friendly — no gym required.

1. Bicep Curls

Target: Biceps
Tip: Keep elbows close to your body and lift slowly for control.

2. Tricep Kickbacks

Target: Triceps
Tip: Bend slightly forward and extend the arms straight back with a squeeze.

3. Shoulder Press

Target: Shoulders
Tip: Press upward in a smooth arc. Avoid arching your back.

4. Lateral Raise

Target: Shoulder caps
Tip: Lift to shoulder height only. Control the down motion.

5. Hammer Curl Hold

Target: Biceps + grip
Tip: Hold the dumbbells in a neutral grip for 10-15 seconds each set.

Repeat each move for 2 sets of 10–12 reps. Use 1kg–2kg Ritual Co dumbbells for best results.
