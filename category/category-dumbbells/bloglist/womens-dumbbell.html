Why Regular Dumbbells Aren’t Always Ideal for Women

  • ✔️ Bulky sizes that strain wrists
  • ✔️ Rough textures that cause calluses
  • ✔️ Weights that don’t suit beginners

Most dumbbells in the market are designed with a one-size-fits-all mentality — often ignoring the ergonomic needs of women who are just beginning or prefer sculpting over bulking.

How to Choose the Right Weight

If you’re just starting, go with 1kg. For those who’ve done some bodyweight or yoga training, 2kg is a good progression. The goal isn’t to lift heavy but to move consistently and safely.

Sample Beginner Workout Using Dumbbells

  • 🧘‍♀️ 10 x Bicep Curls
  • 🧘‍♀️ 10 x Shoulder Presses
  • 🧘‍♀️ 10 x Goblet Squats
  • Repeat 3 rounds — 3x/week

Final Thoughts

Invest in dumbbells that feel good in your hands and look good in your space. Your environment impacts your consistency. Start small, but start beautifully.
